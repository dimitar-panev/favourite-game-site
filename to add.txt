        <h1>The first game in the series is <span style="font-style: italic;">Forza Horizon</span></h1>
        <p>  
            Forza Horizon is a 2012 racing video game developed by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Playground_Games">Playground Games</a> and published by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xbox">Microsoft Studios</a> for the Xbox 360 on 23 October 2012. The game is the fifth instalment of the Forza series,
            having originally spun-off from <a href="https://www.turn10studios.com/">Turn 10 Studios-developed</a> Forza Motorsport titles. Taking place during the fictitious Horizon Festival, a street racing event, the player's aim is to progress via winning races,
            while also increasing their popularity level by performing stunts and activities. Unlike previous games in the Forza series, Forza Horizon takes place in an open world that players can explore. 
       </p>

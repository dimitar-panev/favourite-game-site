About the game

Forza Horizon 5 is a highly accalimed racing video game developed by Playground Games and published by Xbox Game Studios. Released on November 9, 2021, it's the fifth installment in the Forza Horizon seriesand the twelfth main entry in the overall Forza series.

Here are some key points a bout the game:

  • Setting: The game features a vast open-world environment set in a fictionalized representation of Mexico, offering the largest map in the Forza Horizon series, 50% larger than its predecessor.
  • Diverse Landscapes: Players can explore diverse terrains including an active caldera volcano, jungles, beaches, ancient Mayan temples, and cities like Guanajuato.
  • Customization: Extensive customization options are available for both cars and the player character. Players can create custom liveries, tunes, and perform modifications like engine swaps and body kits.
  • Weather System: A new weather system introduces local weather effects, such as dust storms in the dry season and tropical storms during the fall storm season, reflecting the varied climates of Mexico.
  • Ray Tracing: Forza Horizon 5 is the first in the series to support ray tracing on cars, enhancing the visual experience, although this feature is only available in Forzavista.
  • Multiplayer: The game offers a social and connected experience with multiplayer races and a campaign mode where players meet characters and determine the outcome of Horizon Story missions.

The game has recieved critical acclaim for its gameplay, visual upgrades, and the depth of its open-world experience. It also won several aweards, including IGN's Game of the Year award and the three jury-voted awards at The Game Awards 2021. Forza Horizon 5 continues to expand with updates, bringing new content and experiences to players.
